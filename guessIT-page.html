GuessIT


This was the final project for a C++ programming course I took during my senior year of high school. That course was my very first introduction to real programming (I had written programs before, but those were through YouTube tutorials on making games in the Unity engine). Throughout my time in this class, I learned that computer science truly was what I wanted to study in college. Each assignment felt more like a fun challenge, rather than a chore, and I quickly began anticipating the new concepts we'd learn at each class session.






The goal of this final assignment was to recreate a number guessing game demonstrated to us during class. The computer randomly chooses three digits, and the player is tasked with guessing the correct three digit sequence (ideally in 7 guesses or under). It looks quite primitive now, but even so, I'm proud of this little program; it's what made me certain that this could be turned into a real career. For that, I'll always be thankful for this assignment, and the entire introductory course itself.
